Introduction.

Saudi Factory for Environmental Solvents is a leading manufacturer of environmentally responsible solvents, cleaners and specialty products, which are Non WHMIS and Non Regulated (USA).
Our products are used in a variety of industries and settings including Automotive, Aviation, Government, Industry, Institutions, Marine, Mining, Oil Exploration, Pulp and Paper and Transportation perform as directed.

An ISO 14001 & ISO 9001 Registered Company
